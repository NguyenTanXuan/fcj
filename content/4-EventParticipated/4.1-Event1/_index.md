# Summary Report: “AI/ML/GenAI on AWS Workshop”

### Event Objectives

- Provide an overview of the current AI/ML ecosystem in Vietnam  
- Introduce core AWS AI/ML services and their end-to-end capabilities  
- Demonstrate practical workflows for training, deploying, and operationalizing ML models  
- Explore Generative AI solutions using Amazon Bedrock and modern RAG architectures  

### Key Highlights

#### Welcome & Introduction

- Participant check-in and networking opportunities  
- Overview of workshop goals and structure  
- Introductory activity to engage attendees  
- High-level discussion on the AI/ML landscape and adoption trends in Vietnam  

#### AWS AI/ML Services Overview

- **Amazon SageMaker** introduced as a full-stack platform for building, training, and deploying ML models  
- Techniques for **data preparation**, including labeling and preprocessing workflows  
- Overview of **model training, hyperparameter tuning, and deployment** options  
- Built-in **MLOps** tools for automation, monitoring, and lifecycle management  
- **Live demonstration** using SageMaker Studio to showcase hands-on workflows  

#### Break Session

- Short break for refreshments and networking  

#### Generative AI with Amazon Bedrock

- Overview of leading **Foundation Models** (Claude, Llama, Titan) and guidance on choosing the right model  
- **Prompt engineering strategies** including structured prompting, reasoning methods, and few-shot examples  
- Introduction to **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)**, its architecture, and how to integrate Knowledge Bases  
- **Bedrock Agents** demonstrated as tools for orchestrating multi-step tasks and service interactions  
- Discussion on **guardrails**, safety controls, and responsible AI practices  
- **Live demo** showcasing the process of building a generative AI chatbot using Amazon Bedrock  

### Key Takeaways

- AWS offers a complete stack for ML, from data preparation to MLOps automation  
- SageMaker simplifies ML development through integrated tools and managed infrastructure  
- Foundation Models in Bedrock provide flexible options for various generative AI use cases  
- Prompt engineering and RAG patterns significantly enhance model reliability and accuracy  
- Safe AI practices, including guardrails and content filtering, are essential for production deployments  

### Applying to Work

- Utilize SageMaker for end-to-end ML experiments and automating training workflows  
- Apply prompt engineering principles to improve accuracy and consistency of GenAI applications  
- Explore RAG to integrate organizational data into chatbot and knowledge systems  
- Use Bedrock Agents to automate workflows and build multi-step GenAI applications  
- Adopt AWS safety features to ensure responsible AI deployment  

### Event Experience

Attending the AI/ML/GenAI workshop at the AWS Vietnam office offered valuable insights into practical machine learning and GenAI development on AWS.

#### Learning from AWS Experts

- Gained a clear understanding of how AWS services streamline ML and GenAI workflows  
- Real-world demonstrations made complex architectures easier to grasp  

#### Hands-on Exploration

- The SageMaker demo revealed how intuitive end-to-end ML pipelines can be  
- Bedrock demos illustrated how rapidly GenAI applications can be built and deployed  

#### Insights into GenAI Best Practices

- Learned effective prompting techniques and when to use few-shot or chain-of-thought prompts  
- Deepened knowledge of RAG and how it enhances relevance and accuracy for enterprise use cases  

#### Networking Benefits

- Connected with practitioners and AWS staff, exchanging ideas on real-world AI adoption  
- Discussions highlighted challenges and opportunities in Vietnam’s fast-growing AI landscape  

#### Lessons Learned

- Combining SageMaker with Bedrock enables both traditional ML and advanced GenAI workflows  
- RAG and guardrails are critical for reliable, safe AI systems  
- AWS’s integrated tools accelerate development while ensuring compliance and scalability
