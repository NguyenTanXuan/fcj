During my internship, I participated in two events. Each one was a memorable experience that provided new, interesting, and useful knowledge, along with gifts and wonderful moments.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)  
&emsp;**Event Name:** AI/ML/GenAI on AWS  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 08:30 – 12:00, Saturday, November 15, 2025  

&emsp;**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office, Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam  

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Description:** Half-day workshop covering an overview of the AI/ML landscape in Vietnam, AWS AI/ML services including Amazon SageMaker, data preparation, model training, MLOps integration, and Generative AI using Amazon Bedrock. The session included live demos of SageMaker Studio and building a Generative AI chatbot.  

&emsp;**Outcomes:** Gained hands-on experience with AWS AI/ML services, learned practical workflows for model training and deployment, explored Generative AI techniques and prompt engineering, and improved understanding of integrating AI/ML solutions in real-world applications.

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)  
&emsp;**Event Name:** DevOps on AWS  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 08:30 – 17:00, Monday, November 17, 2025  

&emsp;**Location:** AWS Vietnam Office, Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam  

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Description:** Full-day workshop covering DevOps principles, CI/CD pipelines using AWS CodeCommit, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, and CodePipeline. Sessions included Infrastructure as Code (CloudFormation & CDK), container services (Docker, ECR, ECS, EKS, App Runner), and monitoring & observability with CloudWatch and X-Ray. Best practices and case studies on deployment strategies, automated testing, and incident management were presented.  

&emsp;**Outcomes:** Gained hands-on knowledge of AWS DevOps services and CI/CD pipelines, learned IaC tools (CloudFormation/CDK), explored container orchestration and deployment strategies, understood monitoring & observability best practices, and acquired insights into DevOps career pathways and AWS certification roadmap.
